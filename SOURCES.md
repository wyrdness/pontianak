## Film & Popular Culture

### Classic Films
- **Pontianak** (1957). Directed by B.N. Rao. First Malay horror film, established cinematic portrayal.
- **Dendam Pontianak** (Revenge of Pontianak) (1957). Sequel establishing mythology.
- **Sumpah Pontianak** (Curse of Pontianak) (1958). Completing original trilogy.

### Modern Films
- **Kuntilanak** film series (Indonesia, 2000s-present). Modern horror interpretations.
